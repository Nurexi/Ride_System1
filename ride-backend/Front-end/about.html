About Us

Reinventing mobility through fairness, transparency, and choice.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a mobility platform where riders and drivers can interact fairly, transparently, and independently — without algorithmic price manipulation.

We believe that transportation should empower both passengers and drivers. That's why we've built a system where riders set their own price, drivers choose the rides they want, and both parties have complete transparency into every transaction.
