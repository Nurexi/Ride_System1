Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How do I contact my driver?

Once your ride is booked, you'll see your driver's details in the app. You can call or message them directly through the RideFair app's secure communication channel.

How do refunds work?

Refunds are processed based on cancellation time and ride status. Cancellations made before the driver arrives are fully refunded. Refunds typically appear in your account within 3-5 business days.

What safety features are available?

RideFair includes real-time tracking, driver verification, emergency assistance, ride sharing with trusted contacts, and 24/7 safety support. All drivers undergo comprehensive background checks.

How do I report a lost item?

Go to your ride history in the app, select the ride, and report a lost item. Our support team will contact the driver and coordinate item recovery within 24 hours.

Why was my payment declined?

Common reasons include insufficient funds, expired cards, or temporary holds. Update your payment method in app settings or contact your bank for more information.

How do I become a driver?

Visit our Driver Portal, complete the application, pass background checks, and meet vehicle requirements. Our driver support team guides you through each step.

Are promo codes available?

Yes! Check your email for promotional offers or apply promo codes during checkout. Follow our social media for exclusive deals and limited-time offers.

How do I delete my account?

Navigate to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Note this action is permanent and removes all data. Contact support for assistance with account management.
