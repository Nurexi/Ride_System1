During Your Ride

Simple tips to ensure your safety every time you ride

Verify Driver & Vehicle

Always check the driver's name, photo, and license plate matches what's shown in the app before getting in.

Share Your Trip

Use the in-app sharing feature to let friends or family track your ride in real-time.

Sit in the Back Seat

For optimal personal space and safety, we recommend sitting in the back seat.

Contact Support if Needed

If you feel uncomfortable at any point, contact our 24/7 support team immediately.
